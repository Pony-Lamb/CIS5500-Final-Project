Spicy Tonkotsu Ramen

Matched Recipes

  • Authentic Pork Bone Ramen
  • Spicy Miso Broth with Noodles
  • Homestyle Ramen Bowl

Similar Restaurant Dishes

  • Spicy Ramen House
  • Noodle Nest

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pork bone broth
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp miso paste
  • Ramen noodles
  • Chashu pork
  • Green onions

Simmer pork bone broth with sesame oil and miso paste. Boil noodles, then combine and top with chashu pork and green onions.

Directions

  1. Simmer pork bone broth with sesame oil and miso paste over medium heat.
  2. Boil ramen noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Prepare toppings: slice chashu pork, chop green onions.
  4. Combine broth and noodles in a bowl.
  5. Top with chashu pork, green onions, and serve hot.
